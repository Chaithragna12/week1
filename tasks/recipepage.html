Carrot Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots, grated
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 250 grams sugar
  • 100 grams ghee (clarified butter)
  • 50 grams cashews
  • 50 grams raisins
  • 5-6 cardamom pods, powdered

Steps of the Recipe

  1. Peel and grate the carrots.
  2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, add the grated carrots and milk. Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once the milk starts to boil, reduce the heat and cook until the milk is fully absorbed by the carrots. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.
  4. Add sugar and mix well. Continue to cook on medium heat.
  5. In a separate pan, heat ghee and fry the cashews until golden brown. Add raisins and fry for another minute.
  6. Add the fried cashews and raisins to the carrot mixture. Mix well.
  7. Continue to cook until the halva thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  8. Add the powdered cardamom and mix well.
  9. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.

Cooking Terms

Grate: To shred food into small pieces using a grater.
Heavy-bottomed pan: A pan with a thick base that distributes heat evenly and prevents food from burning.
Clarified butter (Ghee): Butter that has been melted and had its water and milk solids removed, leaving a pure fat that can be heated to higher temperatures without burning.
